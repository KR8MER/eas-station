## FAQ

**Q: What happens if I stop paying the annual subscription?**
A: You can continue using the last version you received, but won't receive updates, bug fixes, or support. Your data remains yours.

**Q: Can I switch from annual to perpetual license?**
A: Yes! Annual payments can be credited toward perpetual license purchase (up to 50% of perpetual price).

**Q: Do I need a license for development/testing?**
A: No, AGPL v3 license is free for internal testing. Commercial license only needed for production deployment or closed-source integration.

**Q: What if I outgrow my tier?**
A: Easy upgrades available anytime. Pay the prorated difference for annual subscriptions.

**Q: Is source code included?**
A: Yes, all licenses include full source code. Enterprise licenses grant modification rights without AGPL obligations.

**Q: When will FCC certification be complete?**
A: Target: Q2-Q3 2026. Early adopter licenses will be upgraded automatically at no additional cost.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Annual subscriptions: 30-day money-back guarantee. Perpetual licenses: 90-day money-back guarantee if software doesn't meet documented specifications.
