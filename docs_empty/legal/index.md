# Legal & Policies

Legal information, terms of use, and privacy policy for EAS Station.

## Important Legal Information

!!! danger "FCC Compliance Warning"
    EAS Station generates valid EAS SAME headers and attention tones. Unauthorized broadcast of these signals violates FCC regulations and can result in substantial fines and license revocation.

## FCC Regulations

EAS Station is experimental software. For FCC Part 11 requirements, see:

- [FCC Part 11](https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-47/chapter-I/subchapter-A/part-11) - Emergency Alert System
- [FCC Compliance Guide](../reference/fcc-compliance.md)

## Notable Enforcement Actions

The FCC has issued substantial penalties for EAS tone misuse:

- **2015 iHeartMedia**: [$1 million consent decree](https://docs.fcc.gov/public/attachments/DA-15-199A1.pdf)
- **2014 Multiple Networks**: [$1.9 million settlement](https://docs.fcc.gov/public/attachments/DA-14-1097A1.pdf) for "Olympus Has Fallen" trailer

## Responsible Use

EAS Station is provided for:

- ✅ Laboratory testing
- ✅ Educational purposes
- ✅ Amateur radio experimentation
- ✅ Emergency communications training
- ❌ **NOT for production emergency alerting without proper authorization**
