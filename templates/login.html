Trust & Safety Checklist

This official EAS Station console prioritizes user safety and transparency. Review the quick checklist below before entering credentials:

  • Confirm the domain matches your deployment and the browser reports a valid TLS certificate.
  • Only administrators provisioned inside EAS Station should sign in. Never reuse credentials from other services.
  • Logins are audited, session timeouts are enforced, and repeated failures trigger security alerts.
  • If you suspect phishing or certificate issues, alert your organization immediately using the established incident response channel.
