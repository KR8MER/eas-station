Capture Workflow

  1. Create receivers for each USB dongle with a unique identifier.
  2. Enable Auto-start to let the poller launch tuned listeners.
  3. When the poller hears SAME bursts it requests captures from every enabled receiver.
  4. Monitor signal lock and error states below to diagnose hardware or driver issues.
Built-in drivers: RTL-SDR (RTL2832U) and Airspy via SoapySDR.
